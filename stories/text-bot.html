Text Box

January 15, 2024

How would I tell the world I lost you? How do I tell myself to move on?


How would I live without you, Eniola? Come back to me, please come back to me.


You went out with your friends the night before, but we spoke all night. You wanted to spend time with me because the time we spent apart has been getting to us. Me especially.


Our conversation that night keeps playing in my head. I can't get it out.


You told me about your day, about how much you missed me, about how much you couldn't wait to see me again. You told me about your plans for us, about how you wanted us to grow old together, about how you wanted us to have a family together.


But now, all I have is this text box. This empty text box that I can't fill with your messages anymore. This text box that used to light up my day with your words, your love, your presence.


How do I move on from this? How do I stop looking at this text box, hoping to see your name pop up? How do I stop waiting for messages that will never come?
