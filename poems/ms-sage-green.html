Ms. Sage Green

Dear MS sage green
Brown eyes
Almond shaped
You had your lashes done
Dark brown skin
Your skin smooth like milk
I think about what your name is
You had your hair done in goddess braids that made you look like you were made for that role
The perfectly placed dress on your body
The sage green highlighting your beautifual dark skin and teh whole dress carving out the shape of your body
You didnt have to do much for me to stare
I had zero shame staring at you because i felt i was worthy of it
You looked at me and looked away
you saw me staring and smiled
I wish i said something
Still regretting why I left without saying anything
That smile wokr something in me I thought had died
I hope I see you again
Ms. Sage Green
Darasimi
