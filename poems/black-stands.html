Now Reality

All black for the ones we lost
For the ones their time was cut short by the world
All black for the ones we mourn
For the ones we loved and cared for
For the ones we wished we held on to
For the ones that made a mark on the world
All black for those who never got to make a mark on the world
All black to the ones who left stories worth telling
To the ones who fades away but their memories still stay
All black to the ones we mourn
To the ones we reach out to but realize they are gone
All black to the ones we loved and taught
All black to those that loved and taught
