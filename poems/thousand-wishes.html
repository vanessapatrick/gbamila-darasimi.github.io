I WISH I HAD A THOUSAND WISHES

I wish I had a thousand wishes
I wish you never left me
I wish I could convince you to stay
I wish I could breathe the same air as you
What I would do for just 5 minutes with you
I wish I could stare into your eyes to see your eyes dancing
I wish I could feel your touch on my skin
I wish I never let go of your hand
I wish our last hug was longer than it was
I wished I hugged you till I couldn’t breathe
I wish I was there to clean your tears
I wish you were here to hold me through dark times
I wish I had time to fall in love with you
I wish I wasn’t the villain in our love story
I wish we never stopped trying
I wish I trusted myself
I wish I never hurt you
I wish I never hurt myself
