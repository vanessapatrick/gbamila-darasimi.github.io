Now Reality

They say people come and go
You came at the most random time
But you still found a way to make it yours
The time you came
Everything stopped
And it was just the two of us
I may never find the words beautiful enough
To describe the time in my life you altered
I may need to hace a late night conversation with the moon and aligned that day
But there is so much space between us
So much holding us back
I wish we could live in a fantasy where i dont have to leave things to my imagination
Or we could be in a fantasy where I would be the artist and you would be the art
In a world where you are a painter and i am your muse
Till we can create fantasies into realities
I will hold on to your eyes, your smile,
Laugh, your smell your touch.
That is my NOW REALITY
And I'm not letting it go.
