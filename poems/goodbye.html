Now Reality

I've fallen in love with a stranger
I fell in love with a stranger
I am in love with a stranger
How I don't know you but you affect me in so many ways amzes me
We're on two side of the world
In the same room but different spaces
Side by side but different people
I don’t know why I thought I could know you
So this is me saying good bye
Goodbye to what never started
Goodbye to someone I thought I knew
Good bye to what I thought I needed
Good bye to what I know I needed
